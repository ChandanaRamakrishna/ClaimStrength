# Why Perplexity Comet?

Traditional search → manual fact-checking
❌ 5–10 minutes per claim
❌ Fragmented sources, no context
❌ Hard to follow citation chains

Comet’s agentic browsing:
✅ Finds diverse, credible sources automatically
✅ Follows citation trails to primary research
✅ Understands academic context and taxonomy
✅ ~23 seconds for 6 claims in our demo

**Result:** ~15× faster verification, ~2× more credible sources considered.
